How it works

When you are ready to start learning, simply book a class using my easy online booking tool. If booking for the first time, please also include a message about who you are, what your current English level is, and what you want to achieve from your classes. This information will help me prepare a great class for you!

  • Online classes are held via Skype, Zoom, or Google Meet
  • Materials are shared with Google Docs
  • Regular homework assignments are provided: practice reading, writing, and listening between classes
  • Useful, actionable feedback is given every week, along with resources to help you improve
  • Invoicing is available for business clients
  • A free 30-minute trial class is available to qualified students on request
