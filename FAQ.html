Frequently asked questions

Can I be a student? Anyone is welcome to apply to study with me. I welcome applications from students of any level, any nationality, and any background. If you are 18 or over and have a real desire to learn English, then I would love to help you.

Please note that absolute beginner students should be conversationally fluent in Spanish to facilitate the teaching process.

What do I need to get started? All you need is a free video-chat account. Skype works best for the way I teach, but I can also offer lessons via Zoom and Google Meet. My lessons also sometimes use Google Drive services, but you do not need an account for that: I will send you a free link to access my workspace. You will also need an internet connection that is suitable for video-calling.

How do classes work? At the time of the class, I will call you on the designated video-chat software. Each class will be different but will follow a plan drawn up by me in accordance with your needs as a learner. I love making great classes, but if you want to change anything at any time you are free to do so.

How many lessons do I need? This depends on your goals. Exam preparation might be possible in a month, learning fluent English from scratch can take years. In terms of lesson frequency, normally I recommend two classes a week, although some students may benefit from three classes a week. 

What if my local currency is not euros? The euro price will be converted into your local currency directly; please note that a small commission or exchange rate fees may be required by your payment gateway.

What if there is a problem? If we experience technical issues in the class I will offer to reschedule the class in accordance with the terms of service.

What if I can't attend class? If you are unable to attend a class for any reason, please let the teacher know as soon as possible. If you let me know at least 24 hours before the lesson, I will reschedule the class without charge. If you forget to attend the class and don't let me know, however, I must regrettably charge you for the class. This is because I have had to work hard to make a lesson plan for you, and cannot use this time for another student.

Do you teach classes on Saturdays and Sundays? At present, I do not offer classes on these days owing to external commitments.

Do you teach children? No. My specialization is in teaching adult learners of English, and this is the skill set that I develop and cultivate. Teachers need a special skill set to be successful in teaching children which, through personal choice, I do not work to develop. I can, however, help you find a great teacher for your children, so please let me know if this is the case.

How do I pay you? You may pay by bank transfer, PayPal transfer, or credit/debit card via a PayPal payment gateway. Please note that I require payment in advance for classes to be scheduled.

What if I don’t want to pay in advance? Advance payment is required due to the fact that non-payment would otherwise be unenforceable. In the event that you prefer not to pay for your classes in advance, a service contract that is enforceable in the EU will be required. This option is normally chosen by business or organizational clients. Full legal invoicing is available under Spanish invoicing regulations.
